Tea

Ingredients:

Servings 5
  1. 1 cup water
  2. 1 tbsp tea
  3. 2 cardamom
  4. 2 cup of milk
  5. 4 tbsp sugar
Next
Step 1
Take a saucepan and add water,tea and cardamom.Let it boil for 2 minutes to release the flavour.
Back Step 2
Add sugar and milk in a saucepan and boil again for 2 minutes.Strain the tea through a sieve and directly into cups and enjoy.
