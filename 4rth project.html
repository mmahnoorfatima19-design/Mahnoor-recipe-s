Chocolate almond ice cream

Ingredients:

Servings 7
  1. 1 cup milk
  2. 6 tbsp sugar
  3. chocolate
  4. 1 cup cream
  5. 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  6. 1 cup almondbutter
  7. 2 tbsp vanilla extract
Next

Step 1
Take a bowl and add sugar,cream and cocoa powder.bring the mixture to a rolling bowl and add chocolate and almonds.
Step 2
Once the chocolate has melted mix vanilla extract cool the mixtuer a bit and then blend it to sure to completely smooth.Chill the mixtuer through in the refrigrator.Then freeze it in one hour.Cover with your favourite topping and serve.
