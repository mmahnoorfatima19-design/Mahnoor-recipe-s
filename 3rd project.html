Coffee

Ingredients:

  1. 2 tbsp coffee powder
  2. 2tbsp sugar
  3. 3 tbsp hot water
  4. 1 cup milk
  5. cocoa powder
Next

Step 1
Take a mug, mix coffee powder,sugar and water.Using a spoon stir vigorously for 3-4 minutes until the mixture turn thick, forthy,and lightcolored.
Step 2
Heat 1 cup of milk in a saucepan until it begin to boil.Pour the hot milk into the mug.
Step 3
Stir gently to combine while maintaining forth to top.Garnish with a sprinkle of cocoa powder if desired and enjoy.
