Chocolate cake

Ingredients:

6 servings
  1. 250gm all purpose flour
  2. 200 gm sugar
  3. 4 beaten egg
  4. 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  5. 2 tbsp baking powder
  6. 1 cup butter
Back Step 1
Take a bowl and mix all purpose flour,sugar,egg,cocoa powder,baking powder and butter.
Step 2
Pre heat the oven to 180 degree centigrade.Meanwhile grease and 7-inch round cake tin with baking paper and butter.
Step 3
Now add the mixtuer into tin and take for 45 minutes.After 45 minutes check if the cake is bake properly inserting a toothpick into the center.If it comes out clean , so the cake is ready.Transfer the cake into wire rack and freeze for 1 hour.Cover with your favourits topping.Then slice and serve.
